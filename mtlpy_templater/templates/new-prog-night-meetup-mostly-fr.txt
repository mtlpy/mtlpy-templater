-- EN ––
Are you tired of coding all by yourself? We have a solution for you!

Montréal-Python organizes an evening of hands-on programming exercises with Python. 

That might just be the perfect opportunity to dive in, live with other human beings (although it stays remote): the evening will be held in real-time on both video-conferencing with Jitsi and with live code editing on Google Colab.

On the menu: {topics_long_en}

All of this in Python’s elegant syntax and in Molière's language but also in a cozy and informal ambiance where everyone is welcome. Exchanges in English are totally possible but the evening will be mainly in French. You’ll only need a web browser and your enthusiasm. :-)

In any case, we will talk about code, we will look at code, we will modify code, we will have a good time and will – maybe – make a step forward to change the world.

A link to exercises will be posted here, about one week before the event. We meet on the day of by following this link:
https://pymtl-meet.fjnr.ca/soiree-prog
